# vững vàng / ổn định / vững chắc

*Added: 2026-04*

---

## ổn định

- **Origin**: Sino-Vietnamese, from 穩定
- **Core meaning**: external stability — a state of things being settled, not in flux
- **Emphasis**: absence of disruption; a condition that *could* change
- **Typical collocations**: công việc ổn định (stable job), kinh tế ổn định (stable economy), cuộc sống ổn định (settled life), tình hình ổn định (stable situation)
- **English analogue**: "stable" in the situational sense — "the situation is stable"

---

## vững vàng

- **Origin**: Native Vietnamese; *vững* (firm, not wavering) + *vàng* (reduplicative intensifier)
- **Core meaning**: inner solidity — firmness of character, composure under pressure, unshakeability
- **Emphasis**: a quality built into the *subject*, not a condition that happens to hold
- **Typical collocations**: tâm lý vững vàng (steady mindset), bước đi vững vàng (firm steps), lập trường vững vàng (unwavering stance)
- **English analogue**: "steadfast", "a steady hand" — "she handles pressure well"
- **Note**: *vàng* here is not the word for gold — it is a phonetic reduplicative that reinforces *vững* rather than adding new meaning. This doubling pattern is very common in Vietnamese adjectives.

---

## vững chắc

- **Origin**: Native Vietnamese compound; *vững* (stable, not wavering) + *chắc* (firm, solid, certain — with a physical, tangible quality)
- **Core meaning**: solidly secure, structurally sound, well-founded
- **Emphasis**: structural integrity — the sense that something won't give way when pressed
- **Typical collocations**: nền tảng vững chắc (solid foundation), bằng chứng vững chắc (solid evidence), cơ sở vững chắc (firm basis)
- **English analogue**: "solid", "well-founded", "structurally sound"
- **Note**: *chắc* on its own means firm/certain and often carries a physical connotation (a well-built table, a confident prediction). Compounding with *vững* intensifies and abstracts slightly.

---

## The Triangle

| Word | Domain | Emphasis | English rough equiv. |
|---|---|---|---|
| ổn định | External condition | Not in flux | stable |
| vững chắc | Structure / evidence | Won't give way | solid, well-founded |
| vững vàng | Inner quality / character | Composure, steadfastness | steadfast, a steady hand |

---

## Substitution test

- *nền tảng vững chắc* ✓ — solid foundation (structural)
- *nền tảng vững vàng* ✓ but softer, more poetic
- *bằng chứng vững chắc* ✓ — solid evidence
- *bằng chứng vững vàng* ✗ — odd; evidence doesn't have composure
- *lập trường vững vàng* ✓ — unwavering personal stance
- *lập trường ổn định* ✗ — a stance isn't a condition to be settled
